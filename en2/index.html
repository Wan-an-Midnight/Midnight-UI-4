why going for biking

The DNA of the team
Midnight

There is no need to say much about the love of cycling. The original intention of the club is to gather a group of people who love cycling together for friendly exchanges and communication. The same enthusiasm and the same faith are with us, and it is a wonderful thing to put youth and love into cycling.

Healthy body, strong body is the capital we are proud of and show off, health can not be ignored, in the leisure time of life to take a reasonable exercise, can not only strengthen the body and broaden the horizon, make friends, do a healthy oneself.

Be polite and strict with yourself.

Freedom is what everyone needs for a long time. They need to relax themselves, relax their spirit, have a proper rest, breathe fresh air, ride freely, do not have to think about the project of study and work, feel the pleasure of the wind passing by their ears, and be the most free person in the world.

About Us

湾岸
Midnight

The Midnight bicycle team on the Bay coast was founded on February 22, 2019. With the passage of time, the number of the team has reached 16, and most of the team are high school students, A group of students who are keen to challenge the limit, love cycling, constantly explore and persevere, Constantly improve yourself in riding, break through your limits and challenge the limits you have never challenged. The team members are versatile, not only limited to the love of cycling, but also have different hobbies and skills from others, Even though their studies are heavy, they can't stop these young and energetic teenagers from riding bicycles, Running on mountains, forests and roads.
